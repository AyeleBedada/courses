In the following illustration the big black numbers from 1 to 12 are references to the small numbers each with orange circle backgrounds that are 1 to 12 and are annotating the interface of AutoCad. As you mouse hover on the large numbers, you will be able to see some information about the start interface components. On this interface(start window) some of the services are active and some are not.
