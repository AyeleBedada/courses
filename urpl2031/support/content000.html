Content

Module 1: Introduction of the Course

(1.1)Objective of the course (1.2)Plan and strategy of the AutoCAD course

Module 2: AutoCAD, Autodesk and the Computer Aided Design Industry

(2.1)Brief history of AutoCAD’s emergence and growth in the CAD industry (2.2)Brief insight on application of different scale projects in AutoCAD (2.3)Background on AutoCAD software features among different releases

Module 3: Introductory Discussion on AutoCAD Software background on documentation in autoCAD, interface and practical demonstrations and exercises

(3.1)AutoCAD and BIM (building information modeling) (3.2)Brief study of the Interface and keys of AutoCAD software (3.3)Built-in and custom templates, script files, and blocks (3.4)Creation, modification and plotting AutoCAD interface (3.5)Introduction of working drawings and related documents

Module 4: Professional Application of AutoCAD

(4.1)Introduction of working drawings and related documents in urban planning and generally in the built environment (4.2)Integrated or collaborative work process (4.3)Exchange of data between AutoCAD and other software such as Microsoft excel
